About Us

Reach Your Full Potential, Achieve Your Goals.

At AV Fitness Studio, we are passionate about helping you achieve your fitness goals in a supportive and motivating environment. Led by Aneesh Varghese, a renowned trainer and bodybuilding champion with over 20 years of expertise, our studio offers personalized training, strength conditioning, and holistic wellness programs to suit all fitness levels.

Our trainer’s journey began with a passion for fitness, which evolved into a lifelong commitment to helping others transform their bodies and minds. With certifications in multiple disciplines, including functional training, weightlifting, and nutrition, he brings a wealth of knowledge to every session.

Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned athlete, AV Fitness Studio is committed to providing personalized training plans, professional guidance, and a welcoming atmosphere that inspires you to be your best.
